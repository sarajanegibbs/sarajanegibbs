About Me

I am a UX designer with a desire to create products that make people happy and help them achieve their goals.

With a background in ecommerce, I have experience in delivering customer focused projects, working closely with web development teams. My biggest achievements so far have been managing mobile and desktop site relaunches as well as my involvement in the launch of mobile and tablet apps.

My involvement in these projects helped develop my interest in user centered design and led me to the UX immersive course at General Assembly. On this course I have learnt the importance of keeping the user at the centre of design decisions, practising the skills and methods needed to create products that people love to use and fit their needs.
