ICX4

ICX4 brings data from over 200 sources together in to one entity profile, so that banks can easily gather current and accurate data about their customers and potential customers, in one place. For the delivery of mid-fidelity wireframes of the profiles, I focused on; Information architecture, wireframing based on use cases, user testing and iterating my designs based on user feedback.
